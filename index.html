    What We Carry

    WINDSHIELD WIPERS

    Every vehicle operates best with well designed auto parts. That’s why we carry a wide selection of parts and tools from several manufacturers — to ensure your vehicle is up and running at top performance in no time. Our variety of parts includes our top quality Windshield Wipers that come with different features.

    BRAKE PADS

    The most important thing your car needs is often also the most overlooked. We carry many different types of auto parts because we understand how vital they are to the overall performance of your vehicle. Our Brake Pads are some of our most popular items. Call us to see if we’ve got the brand you need in stock.

    TYRES

    Tyres are the most important part of a vehicle. The vehicle is not a vehicle  without one. So bring we you a huge variety of tyres from different brands and for different vehicles and our expert support for the tyres you buy here.

     

    BATTERY

    Batteries drive your vehicle from starting of the engine to headlights, wipers, ignition, AC. We have a large variety of batteries available.

    AGRICULTURAL EQUIPMENT ON DEMAND

    We provide agricultural equipment on demand. We provide Rotavators as the primary equipment along with other equipment.

    OIL
